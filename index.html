Churchly – Child Safety & Content Standards

Effective Date: November 13, 2025

At Churchly, developed and published by Swiftmindapps, we are deeply committed to protecting the safety and well-being of all users, especially children. These standards outline our firm stance against any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and our broader child safety practices.

1. Zero-Tolerance Policy Toward CSAE

Churchly strictly prohibits the creation, sharing, or distribution of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM), grooming, exploitation, or any behavior that endangers minors. Any such activity results in the immediate removal of the content, suspension of related accounts, and reporting to appropriate law-enforcement authorities.

2. Safe-Use Standards

  • We do not permit any form of sexually explicit or exploitative content involving minors.
  • We prohibit harassment, bullying, or harmful behavior toward children.
  • We restrict access to certain features for underage users when applicable.
  • We encourage parents and guardians to supervise and guide children’s app usage.

3. Developer Responsibility

Swiftmindapps, the developer of Churchly, ensures that all updates, community features, and user interactions comply with Google Play’s Child Safety Standards Policy.

4. Reporting Violations

If you encounter content or behavior that may violate these standards, please report it immediately to our support team:

  • Email: swiftmindapps@gmail.com
  • Subject line: Child Safety Concern – Churchly

5. Continuous Review

These standards are regularly reviewed and updated to remain compliant with Google Play’s Child Safety Standards and applicable global child protection laws.
